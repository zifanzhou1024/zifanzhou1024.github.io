The analysis rests on three specific structural arguments:
1.  **The Bitcoin Lead-Lag Rule:** `$BTC` leads equities. The speaker asserts the "Bitcoin 4-year cycle has completed" and `$BTC` has already entered a bear market.
* *Correlation:* In 2018 and 2021, `$BTC` peaked and rolled over *before* the S&P 500. A similar lag is playing out now.
2.  **Dow Theory Non-Confirmation:** A confirmed divergence exists between the **Dow Industrials** (`$DJI`) and **Transports** (`$DJT`).
* *Historical Precedent:* This specific divergence (days or weeks apart) preceded the 1929 and 1987 crashes, as well as the 2024/2025 tops.
3.  **Fractal Repetition:** The current S&P 500 price action is nearly identical to the "Double Top" structures of early 2022 and early 2025, specifically the behavior of "miniature rising wedges" forming at the second peak.
